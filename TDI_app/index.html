Top States by Durability Score

In order to Indeintify which ones where the states with the highest amount of Swing Counties, I created what I call the Durability Score.

Durability Score = Durabilty by County * (Votes County/ Votes in the State)

This provides an estimation of the importance of the Swinging County and State realtive to the voters' population.
